chahan

Japanese-style fried rice, often with small pieces of vegetables and protein.

ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice,
  • 2 eggs,
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (like bell peppers, carrots, peas),
  • 1/2 cup chopped protein (like chicken, shrimp, tofu),
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce,
  • 1 tsp sesame oil,
  • oil for frying.

instructions

Heat some oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Remove the eggs and set aside. In the same pan, add more oil if needed and saut√© the vegetables and protein until cooked. Add the rice and stir-fry until heated through. Add the soy sauce and sesame oil and mix well. Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and mix. Serve hot.
