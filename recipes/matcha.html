matcha

Matcha is a type of powdered green tea that's traditionally used in Japanese tea ceremonies. It has a vibrant green color and a distinctive, slightly bitter flavor.

service practices

Matcha is traditionally prepared in a tea bowl with a bamboo whisk. The tea is served in a ceremonial manner, and the bowl is often passed between guests.

interesting facts

Matcha is made from shade-grown tea leaves, which boosts their chlorophyll content and gives matcha its bright green color. The tea leaves are ground into a fine powder, which is whisked with hot water to make matcha tea.
