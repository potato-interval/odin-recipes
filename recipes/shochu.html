shochu

Shochu is a distilled alcoholic beverage, typically lower in alcohol content than spirits like vodka or whiskey, but stronger than sake. It can be made from various ingredients, including barley, sweet potatoes, rice, or buckwheat.

service practices

Shochu is often enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or mixed with hot or cold water. It can also be used in cocktails.

interesting facts

Shochu, unlike sake, undergoes a distillation process. Its flavor profile can vary widely depending on the base ingredient used.
