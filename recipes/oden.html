oden

A type of hot pot with various ingredients such as boiled eggs, daikon, konjac, and processed fishcakes stewed in soy-flavored dashi broth.

ingredients

  • 4 boiled eggs,
  • 1 daikon radish,
  • 4 konnyaku blocks,
  • 8 fishcakes,
  • 2 blocks firm tofu,
  • 8 cups dashi,
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce,
  • 1/4 cup mirin,
  • 1/4 cup sake,
  • mustard for serving.

instructions

Cut the daikon into thick slices and boil until tender. Cut the konnyaku and tofu into bite-sized pieces. In a large pot, add dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sake and bring to a simmer. Add all the ingredients and simmer for 2-3 hours. Serve hot with mustard.
