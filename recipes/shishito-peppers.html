shishito peppers

Grilled shishito peppers with a sprinkle of sea salt.

ingredients

  • 200g shishito peppers,
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil,
  • sea salt to taste.

instructions

Toss the shishito peppers in the cooking oil. Grill the peppers until they are blistered and slightly charred. Sprinkle with sea salt before serving.
