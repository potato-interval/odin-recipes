dorayaki

A dessert made of two small pancake-like patties with a filling of sweet Azuki red bean paste.

ingredients

  • 1 cup flour,
  • 1/2 cup sugar,
  • 1 tsp baking powder,
  • 2 eggs,
  • 1/4 cup water,
  • 1 tsp soy sauce,
  • 1 tsp mirin,
  • anko (sweet red bean paste).

instructions

Mix the flour, sugar, and baking powder together. In a separate bowl, mix the eggs, water, soy sauce, and mirin together. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and pour in some batter to form a small pancake. When bubbles appear on the surface, flip the pancake and cook the other side. Repeat with the rest of the batter. Let the pancakes cool, then sandwich a spoonful of anko between two pancakes. Serve at room temperature.
