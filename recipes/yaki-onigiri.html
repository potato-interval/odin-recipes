yaki onigiri

Grilled rice balls often brushed with soy sauce or miso.

ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice,
  • 2 cups water,
  • soy sauce,
  • oil for grilling.

instructions

Cook the sushi rice according to package instructions. Once the rice is cooked and slightly cooled, wet your hands and shape the rice into triangular or round shapes. Brush each rice ball with soy sauce. Heat some oil in a grill pan and grill the rice balls on each side until crispy. Serve warm.
