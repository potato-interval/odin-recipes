mochi

Japanese rice cake made of mochigome, often filled with sweet red bean paste.

ingredients

  • 1 cup mochiko (sweet rice flour),
  • 1/2 cup sugar,
  • 3/4 cup water,
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch,
  • Anko (sweet red bean paste).

instructions

Mix the mochiko, sugar, and water together until smooth. Pour into a heatproof dish and steam for 20 minutes until set. Let cool, then cut into pieces. Dust a clean surface with cornstarch and place each piece of mochi on top. Flatten each piece and place a spoonful of anko in the center. Fold the mochi over the anko and seal. Serve at room temperature.
