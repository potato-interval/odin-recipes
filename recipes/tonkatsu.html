tonkatsu

Breaded and deep-fried pork cutlet.

ingredients

  • 4 pork cutlets,
  • salt and pepper to taste,
  • 1 cup flour,
  • 2 eggs,
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs,
  • oil for frying,
  • tonkatsu sauce for serving.

instructions

Season the pork cutlets with salt and pepper. Dredge in flour, dip in beaten eggs, then coat in panko breadcrumbs. Heat the oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry the cutlets until golden brown. Serve with tonkatsu sauce.
