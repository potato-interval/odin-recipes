tempura

Lightly battered and fried vegetables and shrimp.

ingredients

  • Assorted vegetables (like bell peppers, sweet potatoes, eggplant) and shrimp,
  • 1 cup flour,
  • 1 egg,
  • 1 cup ice water,
  • oil for frying,
  • tempura dipping sauce for serving.

instructions

Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces and devein the shrimp. Mix the flour, egg, and ice water together to make the batter. Heat the oil to 350°F (175°C). Dip the vegetables and shrimp in the batter and fry until golden brown. Serve with tempura dipping sauce.
