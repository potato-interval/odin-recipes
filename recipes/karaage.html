karaage

Japanese-style fried chicken.

ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken thighs,
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce,
  • 2 tbsp sake,
  • 2 tbsp ginger juice,
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced),
  • 1 cup potato starch,
  • oil for frying,
  • lemon wedges for serving.

instructions

Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Mix the soy sauce, sake, ginger juice, and minced garlic together to make the marinade. Marinate the chicken for at least 30 minutes. Coat the chicken in potato starch. Heat the oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry the chicken until golden brown. Serve with lemon wedges.
