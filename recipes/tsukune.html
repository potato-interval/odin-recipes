tsukune

Grilled chicken meatballs with yakitori sauce.

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs,
  • 1 egg,
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce,
  • 1/2 cup mirin,
  • 2 tbsp sugar,
  • bamboo skewers.

instructions

  • Mix the ground chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, and green onions together.
  • Form into small, elongated meatballs and skewer them.
  • Mix the soy sauce, mirin, and sugar together to make the sauce.
  • Grill the skewers, brushing with the sauce frequently, until the meatballs are cooked through.
