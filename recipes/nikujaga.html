nikujaga

Meat and potatoes stewed in sweet soy sauce.

ingredients

  • 200g thinly sliced beef,
  • 4 potatoes,
  • 2 onions,
  • 2 carrots,
  • 1 cup dashi,
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce,
  • 3 tbsp sugar,
  • 3 tbsp sake,
  • 3 tbsp mirin,
  • oil for frying.

instructions

Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the beef until browned. Add the vegetables and sauté until slightly browned. Add dashi, soy sauce, sugar, sake, and mirin and bring to a simmer. Cover and simmer for about 20 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
