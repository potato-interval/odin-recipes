zaru soba

Cold buckwheat noodles served with a dipping sauce.

ingredients

  • 200g soba noodles,
  • 1 cup soy sauce,
  • 1/2 cup mirin,
  • 1/2 cup sake,
  • 1/4 cup sugar,
  • 1/4 cup bonito flakes
  • wasabi and green onions for serving.

instructions

Cook the soba noodles according to package instructions, then rinse under cold water and drain. For the dipping sauce, combine soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, and bonito flakes in a pot and bring to a boil. Once cooled, strain out the bonito flakes. Serve the noodles with the dipping sauce, wasabi, and chopped green onions.
