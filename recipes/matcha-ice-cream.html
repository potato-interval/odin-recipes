matcha ice cream

Green tea flavored ice cream.

ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream,
  • 1 cup milk,
  • 3/4 cup sugar,
  • 2 tbsp matcha powder.

instructions

Whisk the matcha powder in a few tablespoons of hot water to make a paste. Heat the milk, sugar, and matcha paste in a saucepan until the sugar is dissolved. Let cool, then mix in the heavy cream. Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions. Freeze until firm.
