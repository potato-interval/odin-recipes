beer

Beer is extremely popular in Japan, with several major breweries producing a variety of styles, including lagers, ales, and craft beers.

service practices

In Japanese culture, you should never pour your own beer in a social setting. It's customary for others at the table to fill your glass and for you to fill theirs in return.

interesting facts

Beer has been brewed in Japan since the late 17th century, but it really took off in the late 19th century with the foundation of breweries like Kirin and Sapporo. Japan's craft beer scene has been booming in recent years.
